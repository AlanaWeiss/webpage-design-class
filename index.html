COVID-19 is the infectious disease caused by the most recently discovered coronavirus. This new virus and disease were unknown before the outbreak began in Wuhan, China, in December 2019.

ABOUT

Symptoms

The most common symptoms are fever, tiredness, and dry cough. Some patients may have aches and pains, nasal congestion, runny nose, sore throat or diarrhea. These symptoms are usually mild and begin gradually. Some people become infected but don’t develop any symptoms and don't feel unwell.

PREVENTION

How to protect yourself

Wear mask

When you go outside wear a facial mask. It will help you to avoid being exposed to germs. It also will help avoid to contaminate surfaces if you already have the virus.

No vaccine

Currently there is no vaccine to prevent the COVID-19 disease. But there is some things we can do to avoid being exposed to the virus.

Wash hands

Wash your hands couple of time in a hour with water and soap. If you can't, use a 70% hand sanitizer.
